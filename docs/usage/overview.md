## What You Can Do

LogLife provides three main features:

- **[Audio Journaling](audio-journaling.md)** — Send voice notes that get transcribed and summarized
- **[Referrals](referrals.md)** — Share contacts to invite others to LogLife
- **[Managing Goals](managing-goals.md)** — Track, rate, and manage your personal goals

Explore each section to learn more about how to use LogLife effectively!
